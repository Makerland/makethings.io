How it works?

Make Things is all about learning, having fun and meeting new people. We care about people: you're in the center of the event and we will make sure you fell in love with the world of hardware. During the whole day we will have a couple of workshop stations that will be working all the time. It means that you can join a workshop anytime you want and stay for as long as you want. If you want to get a little bit of everything, you can do that. If you want to get a deep dive into one topic, you can do that. No fixed schedule, no boredom: just fun, robots and MAKING THINGS.
