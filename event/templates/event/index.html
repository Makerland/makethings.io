How it works?

Make Things is all about learning, having fun and meeting new people. We care about people: you're in the center of the event and we will make sure you fell in love with the world of hardware. During the whole day we will have a couple of workshop stations that will be working all the time. It means that you can join a workshop anytime you want and stay for as long as you want. If you want to get a little bit of everything, you can do that. If you want to get a deep dive into one topic, you can do that. No fixed schedule, no boredom: just fun, robots and MAKING THINGS.

Makerland

Make Things is a part of bigger community of Makers: Makerland, the biggest European Maker Conference. Makerland takes place every year in Warsaw, Poland, where 300 amazing people come to meet, make and have fun. Makerland aims to make it as easy as possible for people to dive into hardware, really understand technology and start Making Things.

Make Things in your city?

You can have Make Things event in your city, too! Every Make Things event is organized by a local team of volunteers who are passionate friends with a global help from Makerland team. Help us spread the joy of making things and bring Make Things event to your city.
