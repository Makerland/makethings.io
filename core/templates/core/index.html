Mission

We believe that technology is for people. People should be able to fix things they buy, understand how they work and be able to turn their ideas into reality. Technology should makes our lives easier and more joyful. With the rapid popularization of amazing technologies like Arduino, Raspberry Pi or 3D printing, we believe that everyone who is resourceful and creative can be a Maker.

So here is the goal: to make it as easy as possible for people to dive into hardware, really understand technology and start Making Things. We're trying to achieve this by organizing free, one-day workshops, providing resources and tools to local communities and empowering them to become Makers.
